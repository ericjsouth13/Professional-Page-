About The Lovers

The Backseat Lovers is an alternative rock band that formed in Provo, Utah in 2018. This rock quartet consists of Joshua Harmon (Lead Singer and Guitar), Jonas Swanson (Guitar), Juice Welch (Drums), and KJ Ward (Bass).
